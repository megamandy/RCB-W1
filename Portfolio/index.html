About Me


I was first introduced to code when I was in high school and I would pull the source from websites I liked to make my Xanga (blog) more stylish. Before that, in 8th grade I remember coming home from school to watch TechTV.

Now, I'm currently 27 years old and learning to code so that I can mesh art and indie comics into phone apps and video games. In my free time I do freelance illustration and I hope to create games with complex fantasy stories for all ages to enjoy.

My Major Influences: Square-Enix, Marvel Entertainment, DC Entertainment, Hayao Miyazaki.
